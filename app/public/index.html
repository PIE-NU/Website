Who We Are

Pioneers of Interactive Entertainment (PIE) is Northwestern University's chapter of the International Game Developers Association. Our mission is to promote interactive entertainment as a field of study and career option for Northwestern students through networking opportunities and hands-on experiences.

The Expedition

PIE is preparing to embark on a year long expedition into the realm of game creation. Together, we will bring an epic game to life, designing and creating it from the ground up. We are looking for passionate individuals in a variety of disciplines to join our quest. This adventure will provide a challenging, hands-on experience developing artistic and technical skills, and the chance to network with other talented game creators.

General Membership

There are no prerequisites to be a general member, and students of all skills levels are encouraged to join! General members not only attend PIE events and workshops, but have access to PIE’s Slack and Discord servers, where members can ask questions, share advice, and show off their creations!
